# Scalelist CRO Best Practices

## Primary Goal
Get readers to sign up for Scalelist.

**Primary CTA**: "Try Scalelist free" or "Find verified emails free"
**Secondary See pricing" / "View plans"

## CTA Placement
1. After intro (soft mention — bridge to content)
2. Mid-article (after a key insight — natural product connection)
3. End of article (strong, specific CTA)

Never more than one hard CTA per section.

## CTA Copy

**Use**:
- "Try Scalelist free"
- "Find verified emails for your next campaign"
- "Get accurate mobile numbers for your prospect list"
- "Start enriching contacts today"

**Avoid**:
- "Click here" / "Learn more" / "Get started" (too generic)
- "Request a demo" (too high friction)

## Content-to-Conversion Flow
1. **Hook**: Identify the pain (bad data, bounced emails, missed calls)
2. **Educate**: Teach the strategy genuinely
3. **Bridge**: Connect it to Scalelist naturally
4. **CTA**: Low-friction next step

Readers should feel helped, not sold to.
