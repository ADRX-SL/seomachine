# Scalelist Competitor Analysis

## Primary Competitors
Apollo, ZoomInfo, Lusha, RocketReach, Wiza, Findymail, Prospeo, Fullenrich

## Competitor Profiles

### Apollo.io
- **Strengths**: Large database, built-in sequencing, generous free tier
- **Weaknesses**: Data accuracy varies, weak mobile coverage outside US, bloated for enrichment-only needs
- **Scalelist angle**: More accurate and simpler — not a full sequencer you pay for but don't use

### ZoomInfo
- **Strengths**: Massive database, deep integrations, intent data
- **Weaknesses**: Very expensive enterprise contracts, overkill for smaller teams
- **Scalelist angle**: Verified data at a fraction of the cost, no enterprise contract required

### Lusha
- **Strengths**: Easy Chrome extension, decent US coverage
- **Weaknesses**: Limited APAC coverage, inconsistent data quality
- **Scalelist angle**: Stronger APAC coverage, more enrichment entry points

### RocketReach
**Strengths**: Wide industry coverage
- **Weaknesses**: Weak mobile numbers, clunky UI, limited integrations
- **Scalelist angle**: Stronger mobile coverage and cleaner workflow integrations

### Wiza
- **Strengths**: Strong LinkedIn integration
- **Weaknesses**: LinkedIn-only, no CRM or API enrichment, weaker mobile coverage
- **Scalelist angle**: CSV, API, and CRM enrichment give teams more flexibility

### Findymail
- **Strengths**: Good email verification, affordable
- **Weaknesses**: Limited mobile numbers, fewer integrations
- **Scalelist angle**: Adds mobile numbers and multi-source enrichment

### Prospeo
- **Strengths**: LinkedIn Sales Navigator integration
- **Weaknesses**: Email only, no mobile numbers
- **Scalelist angle**: Adds mobile numbers and broader enrichment sources

### Fullenrich
- **Strengths**: Waterfall enrichment across multiple providers
- **Weaknesses**: Less control, more complex setup
- **Scalelist angle**: Direct verified data, transparent pricing, simpler workflows

## Where Scalelist Wins
1. APAC coverage — competitors are US-first
2. Multi-source enrichment — Chrome + CSV + API + CRM
3. Mobile number accuracy — many competitors have weak or US-only mobile data
4. Transparent credit pricing — no surprises
5. Human customer support
6. Right-sized for 10–500 person teams
