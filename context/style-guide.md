# Scalelist Style Guide

## Grammar
- Oxford comma: Always
- Em dashes: Use for asides — like this. No spaces around them.
- Numbers: Spell out one–nine. Numerals for 10+.
- Percentages: Always use % symbol. "40% open rate"
- Contractions: Encouraged. "Don't", "you'll", "it's"
- Exclamation marks: One per article maximum. .

## Capitalization
- Headers: Sentence case. "How to find verified emails" not "How To Find Verified Emails"
- Brand name: Always "Scalelist" — not "ScaleList" or "scale list"
- Job titles: Lowercase generically. "account executives", "sales leaders"

## Formatting
- H1: One per article
- H2: Main sections (appear in table of contents)
- H3: Subsections
- Bullet points: For 3+ unordered items
- Numbered lists: For steps or ranked items
- Bold: Key terms and scannable highlights only — not entire sentences

## What to Avoid
- Filler phrases: "In today's fast-paced world...", "At the end of the day..."
- Hedging: "Kind of", "sort of", "might possibly"
- Passive voice: "Emails are sent" → "Reps send emails"
- Adverb stacking: "Very quickly", "extremely important" — find a stronger verb
